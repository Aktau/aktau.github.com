P.E.R.S.E.U.S

Yea, what should I write about? This is just filler text anyway, let's see, how did that vim plugin for generating lorem ipsum like text go again? Anyway, I have little aspirations as a writer, but I do sometimes get the nagging feeling that I want to write about the things I come across while working on projects or getting myself into a new language or way of programming. Like when I got really into Scala and all but fell in love with its handy combination of functional, object-oriented and procedural programming. I guess you could say it catered to my lazyness because it allowed me an easy way out when I couldn't think (recurse) my way to a purely functional (tm?) solution, but I loved it. With the wisdom of a few months between that experience and now, I also have come to believe that some problems are better expressed in one style than another. For that sake allowing combinations seem like a great thing. No doubt that anything can be written in any Turing-complete language, but too much quote-blood has been spilt over poorly emulating Lisp in C so I won't repeat that here.

Paragraphs are the crux of the matter.
